2023 Awardees

The first scholarship cycle recognized high-performing students from Federal University Lokoja and marked the beginning of the foundation’s commitment to student support and opportunity.

2023 highlights

A strong opening year for the foundation.

The 2023 application round earned selected students free US scholarship application mentorship. The winners stood out through highly competitive applications and strong academic profiles.

  • 10 scholars recognized from Federal University Lokoja.
  • Mentorship support launched for selected candidates.
  • Two exceptional applicants received ₦50,000 each.
