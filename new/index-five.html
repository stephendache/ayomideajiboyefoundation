Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation

Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation (AAF) was established in March 2023 with the vision of creating a world where every student has the opportunity to achieve their full potential through education. Our founder, Ayomide Ajiboye, was passionate about breaking down the barriers that prevent talented and deserving students from pursuing higher education. He envisioned a scholarship foundation that would empower and inspire future generations, and provide access to educational opportunities for all.
