About Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation

We are building a hopeful and practical support system for students with talent, drive, and ambition.

Our story

A foundation born from belief in what education can unlock.

Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation was founded to remove the barriers that stop deserving students from accessing the educational opportunities they have worked hard for. At the center of our story is a simple conviction: talent should not be limited by circumstance.

We support students not just with funding, but with confidence, visibility, and guidance. The goal is not only to help them get through school, but to help them imagine bigger futures and pursue them with courage.

Founder-driven purpose

This mission is personal.

Ayomide Ajiboye’s journey as an alumnus of Federal University Lokoja and a scholarship recipient helped inspire a model of support that now gives back to students walking similar paths.

Meet the Team

Our Mission

To empower and inspire future generations by expanding access to education, reducing financial barriers, and supporting students with opportunities that can change their trajectory.

Our Vision

A world where every determined student has the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of the challenges standing in their way.

What we stand for

Our values shape how we serve.

We want every interaction with AAF to feel hopeful, respectful, and grounded in real impact.
