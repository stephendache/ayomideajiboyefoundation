Our Journey So Far

A short, energetic timeline of how the foundation has grown from vision to visible impact.

Timeline

From idea to action.

Each year has helped shape the foundation into a stronger platform for student support, mentorship, and opportunity.

March 2023

The foundation begins

Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation was established with the vision of helping students pursue their potential through education and support.

2023

First scholarship round launched

The foundation introduced its first application cycle and recognized a competitive group of students from Federal University Lokoja.

2024

Impact deepens

The program continued with selected awardees receiving scholarship mentorship and financial support, showing a more structured support model.

2025

New application energy

The foundation kept the mission active with another scholarship call, maintaining focus on final-year science students and long-term opportunity.

What’s next

More support, stronger systems

The future is about expanding reach, building partnerships, and growing a scholarship ecosystem that keeps changing student stories for the better.

The heartbeat

Why this journey matters.

For many students, the difference between giving up and going forward is one timely opportunity. This foundation exists to be that timely opportunity.

Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation
