Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the foundation, scholarship eligibility, student support, mentorship, donations, and partnerships.

The mission of Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation is to empower and inspire future generations by supporting deserving students with educational access, scholarship opportunities, and mentorship.

The scholarship is focused on registered full-time students at Federal University Lokoja, especially certified 400 level students in the Faculty of Science, based on the criteria presented on the scholarship page.

Applicants are expected to provide academic records, a CV, a statement of purpose, and recommendation letters, alongside any other details requested in the application form.

Recipients are selected through a review of the strength of their application, academic promise, demonstrated motivation, and overall competitiveness within the pool.

Yes. Individuals and organizations can donate, partner, or support the foundation’s mission through funding, collaboration, and outreach support.

Yes. AAF’s support model includes scholarship application mentorship for selected students, helping them prepare for graduate opportunities and related pathways.
