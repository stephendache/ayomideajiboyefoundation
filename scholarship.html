Ayomide Ajiboye Scholarship

The Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation is pleased to announce...

Program overview

Scholarship opportunity for final year students of Federal University Lokoja.

This scholarship aims to support outstanding students who have demonstrated academic excellence and a commitment to making a positive impact in their community.

Key Details at a Glance
InstitutionFederal University Lokoja
Eligible level400 level students
Faculty focusFaculty of Science
Support typeGrant + mentorship
Selection basisAcademic performance
Application windowOngoing for 2026 round

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Be a final year student of Federal University Lokoja.
  • Have a minimum cumulative grade point average (CGPA) of 3.5.
  • Demonstrate financial need.

Scholarship Benefits:

  • A one-time financial award of ₦ 100,000 to top 2 applicants to support tuition fees and academic expenses.
  • Opportunity to participate in free 2 weeks US scholarship application mentorship for top 20 applicants
  • Recognition and networking opportunities with other scholars and professionals.

Application Requirements:

  • Completed scholarship application form.
  • Academic transcripts (100L - 300L)
  • Curriculum vitae (CV)
  • Personal statement
  • Letters of recommendation
Application flow

A clear process that rewards preparation.

Students who apply thoughtfully, prepare their documents well, and tell their story clearly stand a stronger chance.

Step 1: Prepare your documents

Gather transcripts, CV, statement of purpose, and recommendation letters.

Step 2: Submit your application

Complete the form carefully and make your application as clear and strong as possible.

Step 3: Await selection

Applications are reviewed competitively, and selected scholars move into support opportunities.
