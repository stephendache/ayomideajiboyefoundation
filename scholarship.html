Ayomide Ajiboye Scholarship

A scholarship and mentorship opportunity designed to support deserving students, reward excellence, and open bigger doors.

Program overview

Scholarship support for students working hard toward a stronger future.

The Ayomide Ajiboye Foundation scholarship supports students with financial relief and scholarship application mentorship, helping them stay focused in school and prepare for what comes after graduation.

InstitutionFederal University Lokoja
Eligible level400 level students
Faculty focusFaculty of Science
Support typeGrant + mentorship
Selection basisAcademic performance and potential
Application windowOngoing for 2026 round

Who can apply

  • Registered full-time undergraduate at Federal University Lokoja.
  • Certified 400 level student at the time of application.
  • Able to provide proof of completion of 300 level work.
  • Able to submit a Statement of Purpose and CV.
  • Able to provide Letters of Recommendation.

What selected students receive

  • Financial support for top applicants.
  • Scholarship application mentorship for selected candidates.
  • Guidance for graduate school opportunity pathways.
  • Connection to a community that believes in their future.
Application flow

A clear process that rewards preparation.

Students who apply thoughtfully, prepare their documents well, and tell their story clearly stand a stronger chance.

Step 1: Prepare your documents

Gather transcripts, CV, statement of purpose, and recommendation letters.

Step 2: Submit your application

Complete the form carefully and make your application as clear and strong as possible.

Step 3: Await selection

Applications are reviewed competitively, and selected scholars move into support opportunities.
